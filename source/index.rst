JWST Galaxy Analysis documentation
==================================

A small, reproducible package for reading JWST CEERS and UNCOVER DR4 FITS
catalogs, selecting redshift slices, propagating photometric and stellar-mass
uncertainties, and plotting stellar mass versus U-band AB magnitudes.
